About

Looking fresh, made easy.

RefreshMe is an on-demand marketplace that connects you with licensed barbers and stylists in your area — for cuts, styling, braids, beards, and everything in between. Book in the app, show up looking sharp.

What we do

We make it simple to find a pro who's open right now. Browse nearby stylists, see their work, book an appointment, and pay — all in one place. Stylists get a lightweight way to list their services, fill their calendar, and grow their book without wrestling a website.

For customers

Find vetted barbers and stylists near you. Book in minutes. Pay through the app.

For stylists

List your services, manage bookings, and get paid. First month free when you join.

For everyone

Cuts, color, braids, beards, locs, styling — we serve men and women across every texture.

Where we are

RefreshMe is live on Android today and coming to iOS in July 2026. We're starting in Charlotte, NC and expanding across the Carolinas and the Southeast through 2026.

Who's behind it

RefreshMe was founded by PJ (Phill) — a founder based in Charlotte who got tired of the friction between clients and stylists and built the tool he wanted to use. The team is small and moves fast. We ship weekly.

How we make money

Stylists pay a monthly subscription to list their services on RefreshMe (first month is free). We may also collect a small service fee on bookings. That's it — we don't sell your data, and we never will.
