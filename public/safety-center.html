Your Safety is Our Priority

Guidelines, tips, and tools to help you have a safe and positive experience on RefreshMe.

🔒

RefreshMe Safety Guarantee

All Stylists on our platform have been verified and passed basic identity checks before being approved to take bookings. We continuously monitor for policy violations to keep our community safe.

How We Verify Stylists

🪪 Identity Verification

Every stylist submits a government-issued ID that is verified through our identity partner. Unverified stylists cannot accept bookings.

⭐ Ratings & Reviews

After every appointment, customers can leave a rating and review. Stylists with consistently low ratings are reviewed and may be removed from the platform.

📋 Platform Guidelines

All stylists agree to our Community Guidelines and Code of Conduct before joining. Violations are taken seriously and may result in permanent removal.

Safety Tips for Customers
  • Always book appointments through the RefreshMe app — never pay or communicate outside the platform.
  • Check a stylist's rating and reviews before booking. Look for verified badges on their profile.
  • For at-home appointments, let a trusted person know your location and the appointment time.
  • Use the in-app chat to communicate with your stylist. Keep all conversations on the platform.
  • If something feels wrong, trust your instincts. You can cancel a booking at any time before it begins.
  • After your appointment, leave an honest review to help keep the community safe for everyone.
Safety Tips for Stylists
  • Only accept bookings through the app. Never share your personal phone number or address publicly.
  • Review a customer's profile and booking history before confirming an appointment.
  • For mobile appointments, share your schedule with a trusted contact and check in after each visit.
  • Report any customer who makes you feel unsafe — your report is confidential and taken seriously.
  • Keep all payment and communication within the RefreshMe platform for your protection.

Report a Safety Issue

If you experienced or witnessed unsafe behavior, harassment, or a policy violation, please report it immediately. All reports are reviewed by our Trust & Safety team within 24 hours.
