Questions, answered.

Everything pros ask before signing up. If it's not here, hit us when you apply and we'll answer.

How much does it cost to join?

Nothing. No sign-up fee, no monthly, no chair rent. RefreshMe only takes 10% of bookings you actually complete. If you don't work, you don't pay.

Do I need to be licensed?

We follow state regulations. In most states you'll need a current cosmetology or barbering license. We verify before you go live. If you're in a state without licensing requirements for what you do, a strong portfolio works.

Do I bring my own chair and tools?

Yes — you bring your kit. We bring the clients and the platform. Many pros work out of their own home studio, their existing shop, or travel to the client's location.

Can I still work at my shop?

Absolutely. RefreshMe is built to fit around your existing schedule. Go live on slow days, after hours, or weekends. Many pros use it to fill empty slots.

How do I get paid?

Clients pay in-app. We route 90% to your connected bank account on your chosen payout schedule (daily, weekly, or instant). Tips are 100% yours and passed straight through.

What about insurance and safety?

Every client on RefreshMe is verified. You control who you accept and where. We're working on liability insurance partnerships for pros — details coming before iOS launch.

When does iOS launch?

July 2026. Android is live now, so you can start taking bookings today. Apply below and we'll get you onboarded for the app you prefer.
