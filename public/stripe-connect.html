Set Up Stripe Payouts

Connect your bank account securely through Stripe to receive automatic payouts for your bookings.

1
Open the RefreshMe app and go to Stylist Dashboard → Manage Payouts
2
Tap Set Up Stripe Account to start the secure onboarding flow
3
Enter your bank details — Stripe handles all payment security
4
Once verified, payouts land in your account automatically after each booking
